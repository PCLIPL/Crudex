Diesel Exhaust Fluid

  • Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) is a blended aqueous urea solution of 32.5%
    high purity urea and 67.5% deionized water.
  • DEF is used to reduce pollution in newer BS4 and BS6 diesel engines
  • DEF is introduced into the exhaust gas to neutralize harmful nitrogen
    oxides (NOx) before they enter the atmosphere.
  • DEF has a shelf life of 12 months (or longer) which varies based on the
    temperature itâ€™s stored in.
  • DEF fluids have long been used in on-highway truck engines, and more
    recently in off-road mining and construction equipment
